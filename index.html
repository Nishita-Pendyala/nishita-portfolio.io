About Me

I am a results-driven Software Engineer with a specialization in Data Engineering, dedicated to leveraging technology to solve complex problems and drive business growth. With a solid foundation in programming languages such as C, C++, Java, and Python, coupled with expertise in databases including Oracle, MySQL, and Teradata, I bring a versatile skill set to the table.

My professional journey began at Tech Mahindra Private Limited, where I honed my skills as a Data Engineer for a prestigious US client. During my tenure, I played a pivotal role in managing ETL development and testing for Booking Transactions, ensuring seamless deployment and production readiness. I excelled in overseeing the development cycle for every release, from initial development to quality analysis and final production deployment. My hands-on experience in deployment activities, including code changes, table maintenance, and script development, has equipped me with a keen understanding of data engineering processes.

In addition to my professional experience, I have a strong academic background, including notable projects such as the development of BVRIT Schedule, an Android app centralizing faculty contact information, and the creation of a Housing Portal Website facilitating seamless connections between property owners and tenants.

Driven by a passion for innovation and a commitment to excellence, I am constantly seeking opportunities to further expand my skills and contribute to impactful projects in the field of data engineering and software development.
